How to Use This Book

This book is a playbook for forward-thinking educators who want to bridge academia and the creative workforce, and for design students and young designers looking for real guidance on how to forecast and prepare for the current creative industry.

This book is organized in five parts, each building on the one before it.

Part I, Why, makes the case for urgency: why academia’s pace no longer matches the pace of the creative industry.

Part II, What, is the framework itself: the Five Pillars of Creative Intelligence, which is the core pedagogy of Creative Technology & Design, the AI-Assisted Design concentration, the course structure, and the Cognitive-Process Assessment (CPA).

Part III, Whom, is the evidence: student work and conversations with industry leaders that prove this isn’t theoretical.

Part IV is the Field Guide, the replicable framework. It’s written for you to adapt, not admire.

Every framework in this book, the Five Pillars, the CPA, the concentration structure, is a starting point, not a finished product. We built it around a teaching philosophy and a firm belief in bridging academia and the creative workforce, based on our professional experience running an award-winning design agency and shaping curriculum from inside academia.
